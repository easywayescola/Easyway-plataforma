Story Time 🎬

🎥 A Great Movie Night

I watched an amazing action movie at the cinema with my friends. We bought our tickets online — very easy!

At the theater, we bought the same size of popcorn and candy too. We ate all the popcorn during the movie!

The movie was fantastic! The plot was interesting. The special effects on the big screen were incredible! My friend Sarah was very happy. I was happy too!

After the movie, we watched the trailer for the next film. We ate pizza at a restaurant. The pizza was delicious! Sarah bought the sequel book at the store.

It was a perfect movie night!

❓ Comprehension Questions

1. How did they buy the tickets?

✅ They bought the tickets online.

2. What did they eat at the cinema?

✅ They ate popcorn and candy.

3. What was the movie about?

✅ It was an action movie. The plot was interesting and the special effects were incredible.

4. What did Sarah buy at the store?

✅ She bought the sequel book.
