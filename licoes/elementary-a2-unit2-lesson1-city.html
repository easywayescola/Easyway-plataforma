Reading

Story Time

🏙️ A Perfect Sunday in the City

Last Sunday, I walked around my neighborhood with my friend Leo. We took a map and visited three different streets near the park.

In the morning, we walked to the old bridge downtown. Leo took many photos. The bridge was beautiful! We found a small museum near the corner. We visited it and took some photos inside too.

In the afternoon, we walked to the big park. We found a fantastic food truck near the entrance. We took our food and sat on the grass.

At night, Leo took the bus home. I walked back to my block. It was a perfect Sunday!

❓ Comprehension Questions

1. Where did the narrator walk in the morning?

✅ To the old bridge downtown.

2. What did Leo do at the bridge?

✅ He took many photos.

3. What did they find near the park entrance?

✅ A fantastic food truck.

4. How did Leo go home?

✅ He took the bus.
