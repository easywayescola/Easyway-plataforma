Reading

Story Time 📖

🛍️ The Best Sale Ever

Last Friday, my sister and I needed new clothes for a party. We went to the shopping center in the afternoon. It was a huge sale at our favorite store!

My sister tried five different dresses in the fitting room. She needed a small size. I tried two jackets and paid for the blue one. The price was great because of the sale!

At the cashier, I paid with cash and the lady gave me the receipt and the change. My sister spent more money than me — she needed shoes too!

In the evening, we tried our new clothes at home. We were so happy. It was the best sale ever!

📝 Grammar Note: "The blue one" — "one" replaces the noun "jacket" to avoid repeating it. | "gave me" = me deu

❓ Comprehension Questions

1. Why did they go shopping?

✅ They needed new clothes for a party.

2. How many dresses did the sister try?

✅ She tried five different dresses.

3. How did the narrator pay?

✅ The narrator paid with cash.

4. What did the sister buy besides clothes?

✅ She also bought shoes.
