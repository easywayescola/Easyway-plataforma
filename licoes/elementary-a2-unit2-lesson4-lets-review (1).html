Review Reading

Story Time 📖

🗺️ Last Weekend Was Unforgettable!

In the morning, I left home early with my friend Ana. We needed new clothes for a trip, so we walked to the shopping center. Ana tried many jackets and paid with cash. I spent a little money on shoes. We found a great sale and took some photos!

In the afternoon, we walked to the old neighborhood near the bridge. Ana saw a beautiful street market and we visited every corner. I took photos of the old buildings.

At night, we arrived at a small hotel near the airport. The next day, we traveled to the coast. We saw amazing views from the bus.

We arrived home two days later. I needed a long nap! It was an unforgettable weekend!

📝 New Words:   nap = soneca  |  coast = litoral / costa  |  unforgettable = inesquecível  |  street market = feira livre

❓ Comprehension Questions

1. Why did they go to the shopping center?

✅ They needed new clothes for a trip.

2. What did Ana buy?

✅ Ana tried many jackets and paid with cash (she bought jackets).

3. What did they do in the old neighborhood?

✅ They visited the street market, every corner, and took photos of old buildings.

4. Where did they travel to?

✅ They traveled to the coast.
