Story Time 📖

🏠 A Special Sunday Lunch

Last Sunday, my aunt and uncle visited us for lunch. My cousin Ana arrived too. It was so good to meet everyone again!

In the morning, my mother talked to my aunt on the phone. My father helped my mother in the kitchen. I helped too — I gave the table a nice decoration with flowers.

When Ana arrived, she gave me a big hug and a gift. We talked and talked in the living room. My uncle talked to my father about his job.

At lunch, my mother introduced her new coworker to everyone. She met him last week at the office. He gave us a delicious cake!

After lunch, my cousin helped me with the table. We talked about our plans for the holidays. My aunt gave me a warm hug before she left. It was a perfect Sunday!

📝 Grammar Note: "introduced" = apresentou  |  "She met him" — "him" is the object pronoun for "he". It means she met that man.

❓ Comprehension Questions

1. Who visited the narrator last Sunday?

✅ The narrator's aunt, uncle and cousin Ana visited.

2. What did Ana give the narrator?

✅ Ana gave a big hug and a gift.

3. Who did the mother introduce at lunch?

✅ She introduced her new coworker to everyone.

4. What did the coworker bring?

✅ He gave them a delicious cake.

The Best Birthday Surprise 🎂
Last Saturday, I went to my best friend Clara's house. It was her birthday! It was a surprise party!
Clara's sister talked to everyone before Clara arrived. She gave us balloons and candles for the house. We helped with everything!
When Clara arrived, everyone gave her a big smile. She was so happy! Her mother gave her a beautiful cake with twenty-five candles. We all sang Happy Birthday together.
After the cake, Clara's cousin gave a short concert with his guitar. All the guests talked and ate. Clara was so grateful.
Clara talked to us all about her plans for next month. It was the best birthday party ever!

📖 Story Time
The Worst Monday Ever 😩
Last Monday was the worst day! Everything went wrong.
In the morning, I felt very tired. I forgot my keys inside the apartment and the door was shut. I lost twenty minutes trying to solve the problem. I felt so upset!
Then I missed my bus. I arrived late at the office. My coworker felt bad for me and helped me. I forgot my wallet at home too, so my coworker paid for my coffee.
In the afternoon, I missed an important appointment because I forgot to write it in my calendar. My boss felt upset. I felt terrible and had a headache.
At night, I lost my phone for one hour. I felt so stressed! Then I found it under the sofa. I felt relieved. Bad days happen to everyone. The next day was much better!

Let's Review! – Unit 3

📖 Story Time – Review
Last Weekend – Good & Bad! 🎉😰
On Saturday morning, I felt excited because we celebrated my cousin's birthday. His sister invited twenty guests. I helped with the decorations and gave balloons to everyone. We danced and sang in the afternoon. My cousin met his new neighbors at the party and talked to them for hours.
But in the evening, something went wrong. I lost my wallet at the party. I felt very upset. I missed my last bus and forgot to call my mother. She felt worried.
On Sunday, my neighbor helped me find the wallet. We talked and talked. He met my cousin and gave him a late birthday gift. In the afternoon, I finally found the wallet under a chair! I felt so relieved. Bad days happen, but good people help us!
