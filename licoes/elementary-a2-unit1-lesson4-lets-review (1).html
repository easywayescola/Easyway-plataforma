🎬 An Amazing Weekend!

Last weekend was amazing!

Yesterday, I went to the cinema with my friend Ana. We bought two tickets online. The screen was incredible! We ate popcorn and candy during the movie. Ana bought the same size of popcorn as me!

The movie was an action film. The plot was very interesting. The special effects were incredible! We watched the trailer for the sequel too. Ana was very happy!

After the movie, we went to a restaurant. We ate delicious food and drank lemonade. The food was amazing!

We bought the sequel book at the store. It was an amazing weekend!

* as = que (comparação) — "the same size as me" = do mesmo tamanho que eu

📝 Comprehension Questions

1. Where did the narrator go last weekend?

2. What did they buy online?

3. What did they eat during the movie?

4. How was the plot?

5. What did they do after the movie?
