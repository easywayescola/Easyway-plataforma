Present Past Example

Reading

Story Time 📖

🧳 My First International Trip

Last summer, I traveled to Argentina with my parents. We left home very early in the morning. My father saw our passports and luggage near the door.

We found our gate and waited. I saw many people with big suitcases. The boarding pass was on my phone!

We arrived in Buenos Aires in the afternoon. The city was amazing! We saw beautiful old buildings and parks. We traveled by bus to our hotel.

The next day, we left the hotel early and saw the famous market. My mother saw a beautiful painting and bought it.

We left Argentina after five days. I saw so many incredible things. I want to travel again!

❓ Comprehension Questions

1. Where did the narrator travel to?

✅ The narrator traveled to Argentina.

2. What did they check before leaving home?

✅ They checked their passports and luggage.

3. How did they travel to the hotel?

✅ They traveled by bus to the hotel.

4. What did the mother buy at the market?

✅ She bought a beautiful painting.
