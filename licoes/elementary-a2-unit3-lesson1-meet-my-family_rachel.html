Story Time 📖

🏠 A Special Sunday Lunch

Last Sunday, my aunt and uncle visited us for lunch. My cousin Ana arrived too. It was so good to meet everyone again!

In the morning, my mother talked to my aunt on the phone. My father helped my mother in the kitchen. I helped too — I gave the table a nice decoration with flowers.

When Ana arrived, she gave me a big hug and a gift. We talked and talked in the living room. My uncle talked to my father about his job.

At lunch, my mother introduced her new coworker to everyone. She met him last week at the office. He gave us a delicious cake!

After lunch, my cousin helped me with the table. We talked about our plans for the holidays. My aunt gave me a warm hug before she left. It was a perfect Sunday!

📝 Grammar Note: "introduced" = apresentou  |  "She met him" — "him" is the object pronoun for "he". It means she met that man.

❓ Comprehension Questions

1. Who visited the narrator last Sunday?

✅ The narrator's aunt, uncle and cousin Ana visited.

2. What did Ana give the narrator?

✅ Ana gave a big hug and a gift.

3. Who did the mother introduce at lunch?

✅ She introduced her new coworker to everyone.

4. What did the coworker bring?

✅ He gave them a delicious cake.
