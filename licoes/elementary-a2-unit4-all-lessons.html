📖 Story Time
The Big Match ⚽
Last Saturday was the big match! My team played against the champions. We arrived at the stadium early. The coach talked to everyone.
In the first part, we ran a lot but the score was zero. I kicked the ball three times. My friend Tom played very well. The referee was very fair.
In the second part, Tom kicked the ball and we scored! The stadium was so loud! We ran and ran. We felt so tired but happy.
At the end, we won the match! The coach gave us the trophy. We were the new champions! It was the best day ever!

Exam Week 📝
Last week was exam week at school. Every day was busy!
On Monday, I studied for the math exam. I wrote all the formulas in my notebook. I learned many new things.
On Tuesday, I went to the library. I read three chapters of the history book. My friend Lisa studied with me. We learned together.
On Wednesday was the big math exam. I wrote all the answers. I felt nervous but ready!
On Thursday, the teacher gave us our grades. I learned that I did very well! Lisa won the best grade in the class. We were so happy! Now I learned that studying is very important!

📖 Story Time
The Perfect Lazy Weekend 😴
Last weekend was perfect for relaxation!
On Saturday morning, I woke up at eleven. No alarm! I felt so rested. I listened to my favorite playlist while I ate breakfast.
In the afternoon, my friend Loren called me. We talked for one hour. She invited me to a concert at night. I was so excited!
At the concert, we listened to amazing music. The rhythm was fantastic! I learned all the lyrics of the new songs.
On Sunday, I slept until noon. I took a long nap in the afternoon too. My pillow was so comfortable! I called my mom before bed. She asked about my weekend. I told her it was the most relaxing weekend ever!

Let's Review! – Unit 4

📖 Story Time – Review
My Busy Week 🗓️
Last week was very busy but fun! On Monday morning, I woke early. I studied for my math exam in the library. I wrote many notes in my notebook. I learned so much!
On Wednesday, my team played a big soccer match. We ran a lot on the field. I kicked the ball twice and we won! The coach gave us all a trophy. We felt like champions!
On Friday night, my friend called me. We went to a concert together. I listened to my favorite band. The music was incredible! I learned all the lyrics by the end.
On Saturday, I slept until noon. I took a long nap in the afternoon. I was so tired from the week! I woke in the evening and read a book. It was a perfect mix of study, sports and fun!
