Reading

Story Time

🍽️ Dinner at Luigi's

Yesterday, I went to Luigi's Italian Restaurant with my family. The restaurant was beautiful and the waiter was very friendly.

The menu was beautiful! My father cooked at home every day, but yesterday was special — no cooking! I liked the pizza on the menu. My sister liked the pasta. My mother liked the dessert section!

We bought our food. The waiter was fast. I ate a delicious pizza with cheese. My sister ate spaghetti. The food was amazing! We drank lemonade and water.

For dessert, we ate chocolate cake. It was sweet and delicious! I liked everything at Luigi's. The chef was very talented.

We bought the chef's cookbook at the restaurant. Now my father cooks Italian food at home too!

❓ Comprehension Questions

1. Where did the narrator go yesterday?

✅ To Luigi's Italian Restaurant.

2. What did the narrator eat?

✅ A delicious pizza with cheese.

3. What did they drink?

✅ Lemonade and water.

4. What did they buy at the restaurant?

✅ The chef's cookbook.

Speaking

At the Restaurant

Waiter Good evening! What would you like?
Person 1 I liked the pasta on the menu. I'd like that, please.
Waiter Great choice! And to drink?
Person 1 Orange juice, please.
Waiter Perfect! And you?
Person 2 I went to an Italian restaurant last week. I ate pizza. The same, please!
Waiter Excellent! We have pizza on the menu today. And you, sir?
Person 3 I'd like a hamburger, please.
Waiter Sure! And to drink?
Person 3 A soda, please.
Waiter Great! I'll be right back with your orders.
