He / She / It Rules

Present Simple – He/She/It
+ S
play → plays
eat → eats
run → runs
Most verbs

+ ES
watch → watches
wash → washes
go → goes
-ch, -sh, -ss, -x, -o

Y → IES
study → studies
cry → cries
Consonant + Y

Irregular
have → has
do → does
go → goes

Listening

Listen to Maria 🎧

Maria's Daily Routine

Listen and answer True (T) or False (F):

Maria says:

"Hi! My name is Maria. I wake up at 6:30 AM every day. I take a shower and get dressed quickly. Then I have breakfast with my sister — not my whole family, just my sister!

I go to school by bus. I usually arrive at 7:30. In the afternoon, I do my homework before dinner. I never do homework in the morning — I'm too tired!

In the evening, I watch TV with my family. I usually go to bed at 10 PM."

1. Maria wakes up at 6:30 AM.
2. She has breakfast with her family.
3. She goes to school by bus.
4. She does homework in the morning.
5. She watches TV before dinner.
