🐱 The Little Cat - Unit 1

Ouça a História

Clique para ouvir a narração completa com pronúncia natural

💬 Diálogos Principais

Whiskers (o gatinho):
"Hello! What is your name?"
(Olá! Qual é seu nome?)
Max (o cachorro):
"My name is Max. Do you want to play with me?"
(Meu nome é Max. Você quer brincar comigo?)
Whiskers:
"Yes! Let's be friends!"
(Sim! Vamos ser amigos!)
Max:
"Great! We can play together every day."
(Ótimo! Podemos brincar juntos todos os dias.)

Once upon a time, there was a little cat named Whiskers. Whiskers was very curious and loved to play. Every morning, Whiskers would wake up and look out the window.

One sunny day, Whiskers saw a butterfly in the garden. "What a beautiful butterfly!" said Whiskers. He jumped out the window and started to chase the butterfly.

The butterfly flew over the fence, and Whiskers followed. In the garden next door, he met a little dog named Max. "Hello! Do you want to play with me?" asked Max.

"Yes! Let's be friends!" said Whiskers. From that day on, Whiskers and Max played together every day. They became the best of friends.

And they lived happily ever after.

📚 Vocabulário Importante

Whiskers - Bigodes (nome do gato)
Curious - Curioso
Butterfly - Borboleta
Garden - Jardim
Friends - Amigos
Happily - Felizmente

❓ Compreensão de Leitura

1. What is the name of the little cat?

2. What did Whiskers see in the garden?

3. Who did Whiskers meet next door?

4. What is the main message of this story?

👫 My Best Friend - Unit 1

Ouça a História

Clique para ouvir a narração completa com pronúncia natural

💬 Diálogos Principais

Emma:
"Hi Sarah! Do you want to go to the park?"
(Oi Sarah! Você quer ir ao parque?)
Sarah:
"Of course! I love spending time with you."
(Claro! Eu amo passar tempo com você.)
Emma:
"You are my best friend. I am very lucky!"
(Você é minha melhor amiga. Sou muito sortuda!)
Sarah:
"You are my best friend too! Let's have fun!"
(Você é minha melhor amiga também! Vamos nos divertir!)

My name is Emma, and I want to tell you about my best friend, Sarah. We have been friends since we were five years old.

Sarah is very kind and funny. She loves to laugh and make jokes. We like to spend time together at school and after school.

Every Saturday, we go to the park. We play on the swings, slide down the slide, and sometimes we have a picnic. Sarah always brings delicious cookies that her mother makes.

Last week, I was sad because I did poorly on a test. Sarah came to my house and said, "Don't worry, Emma. You are very smart. Next time you will do better!" Her words made me feel better.

I am very lucky to have a friend like Sarah. She is always there for me. I hope we will be friends forever.

📚 Vocabulário Importante

Best friend - Melhor amigo(a)
Kind - Gentil
Funny - Engraçado
Picnic - Piquenique
Worried - Preocupado
Lucky - Sortudo

❓ Compreensão de Leitura

1. How long have Emma and Sarah been friends?

2. What do they do every Saturday?

3. Why was Emma sad last week?

4. What makes a good friend?

🌳 A Day at the Park - Unit 2

Ouça a História

Clique para ouvir a narração completa com pronúncia natural

💬 Diálogos Principais

Tom:
"Look! The park is beautiful today!"
(Olha! O parque está lindo hoje!)
Lisa:
"Yes! Let's go to the playground!"
(Sim! Vamos ao parque infantil!)
Amy:
"Hi! Do you want to swing with me?"
(Oi! Você quer balançar comigo?)
Lisa:
"Sure! Let's have fun together!"
(Claro! Vamos nos divertir juntos!)

It was a beautiful Saturday morning. The sun was shining, and the sky was blue. Tom and his sister Lisa decided to go to the park with their parents.

When they arrived at the park, they saw many children playing. Tom ran to the playground and climbed the big slide. "This is fun!" he shouted.

Lisa went to the swings. A girl named Amy was sitting on the next swing. "Hi! Do you want to swing with me?" asked Amy. "Yes!" said Lisa, and they became friends.

After playing, Tom and Lisa were hungry. They sat on a bench with their parents and had a picnic. They ate sandwiches, fruit, and drank juice.

In the afternoon, they played ball games and ran around. When the sun started to go down, they went home. Tom and Lisa were very tired but very happy.

📚 Vocabulário Importante

Park - Parque
Playground - Parque infantil
Slide - Escorregador
Swing - Balanço
Hungry - Com fome
Tired - Cansado

❓ Compreensão de Leitura

1. What was the weather like?

2. Who did Lisa meet at the park?

3. What did they eat for lunch?

4. How did Tom and Lisa feel at the end of the day?

🌈 The Rainbow - Unit 3

Ouça a História

Clique para ouvir a narração completa com pronúncia natural

💬 Diálogos Principais

Sophie:
"Mom! Dad! Look at the rainbow!"
(Mãe! Pai! Olhem o arco-íris!)
Mom:
"It's beautiful, isn't it? Each color is special."
(É lindo, não é? Cada cor é especial.)
Sophie:
"I want to follow the rainbow!"
(Quero seguir o arco-íris!)
Dad:
"That's a great idea! Let's go together!"
(Que ideia ótima! Vamos juntos!)

After a heavy rain, a beautiful rainbow appeared in the sky. A little girl named Sophie looked out her window and saw it. "Mom! Dad! Look at the rainbow!" she called.

Sophie decided to follow the rainbow. She put on her boots and went outside. As she walked, she noticed that each color of the rainbow was special.

The red part reminded her of apples and strawberries. The orange part was like the sunset. The yellow part was as bright as the sun. The green part was like the grass and trees.

The blue part was like the sky and the ocean. The purple part was mysterious and magical. Sophie felt happy looking at all the beautiful colors.

When she reached the end of the rainbow, she found a pot of golden coins. But more importantly, she learned that rainbows are nature's beautiful gift to us. From that day on, Sophie always looked for rainbows after the rain.

📚 Vocabulário Importante

Rainbow - Arco-íris
Rain - Chuva
Color - Cor
Sunset - Pôr do sol
Magical - Mágico
Gift - Presente

❓ Compreensão de Leitura

1. What did Sophie see after the rain?

2. What did the red color remind Sophie of?

3. What did Sophie find at the end of the rainbow?

4. What is the main lesson of this story?

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 My Family - Unit 4

Ouça a História

Clique para ouvir a narração completa com pronúncia natural

💬 Diálogos Principais

Lucas:
"My family is very important to me."
(Minha família é muito importante para mim.)
Father:
"We love you very much, Lucas."
(Nós te amamos muito, Lucas.)
Mother:
"Family is the most important thing in life."
(Família é a coisa mais importante da vida.)
Julia (sister):
"I love my family! You are the best!"
(Eu amo minha família! Vocês são os melhores!)

My name is Lucas, and I want to tell you about my family. My family is very important to me.

My father is a doctor. He works hard to help people. He is kind and patient. He teaches me to be honest and responsible.

My mother is a teacher. She loves to read and write. She is creative and fun. She teaches me to be curious and to never stop learning.

I have a younger sister named Julia. She is six years old. She is funny and playful. Sometimes she is annoying, but I love her very much.

My grandmother lives with us. She makes delicious food and tells wonderful stories about the past. I love listening to her stories.

Every Sunday, my whole family gets together. We cook together, play games, and laugh. These are my favorite moments. My family is my greatest treasure.

📚 Vocabulário Importante

Family - Família
Doctor - Médico
Teacher - Professor
Honest - Honesto
Grandmother - Avó
Treasure - Tesouro

❓ Compreensão de Leitura

1. What is Lucas's father's job?

2. How old is Julia?

3. Who lives with Lucas's family?

4. What does Lucas's family do every Sunday?

🗺️ The Adventure - Unit 5

Ouça a História

Clique para ouvir a narração completa com pronúncia natural

💬 Diálogos Principais

Jake:
"Let's explore the forest!"
(Vamos explorar a floresta!)
Maria:
"Great idea! I love adventures!"
(Ótima ideia! Eu amo aventuras!)
Jake:
"Look! A river! Let's follow it!"
(Olha! Um rio! Vamos segui-lo!)
Maria:
"Wow! This is amazing! A waterfall!"
(Uau! Isto é incrível! Uma cachoeira!)

Jake and his friends decided to go on an adventure. They wanted to explore the forest near their town. They packed their backpacks with snacks, water, and a map.

As they walked into the forest, they saw tall trees and beautiful flowers. They heard birds singing and felt the cool breeze. Everything was peaceful and quiet.

After walking for an hour, they found a small river. "Let's follow the river!" said Jake. They walked along the river and discovered a hidden waterfall.

"Wow! This is amazing!" said his friend Maria. They sat by the waterfall and had lunch. They talked and laughed together.

On the way back, they took a different path. They got a little lost, but they used their map to find their way home. When they arrived home, they were tired but very happy.

Jake realized that the best adventures are the ones you share with friends. He couldn't wait for their next adventure.

📚 Vocabulário Importante

Adventure - Aventura
Forest - Floresta
River - Rio
Waterfall - Cachoeira
Amazing - Incrível
Lost - Perdido

❓ Compreensão de Leitura

1. Where did Jake and his friends go?

2. What did they discover after walking for an hour?

3. What did they do by the waterfall?

4. What did Jake learn from this adventure?
